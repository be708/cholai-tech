About Cholai Tech

Cholai Tech is Papua New Guinea’s first AI-powered web design studio. We build fast, mobile-first websites for PNG businesses, creators, and communities.

Why We Exist

Papua New Guinea has 9 million people and 800+ languages. Yet only 11% of our population is online. That means 8 million people are waiting for their first website, first online store, first digital opportunity.

Connectivity is arriving in PNG through fiber and satellites like Starlink. Cholai Tech exists to make sure PNG businesses are ready to use it.

What We Do

  • Web Design: Mobile-first websites that load fast on Digicel and bmobile data
  • E-Commerce: Online stores with BSP, Kina Bank, and cash-on-delivery options
  • AI Tools: Business assistants that speak English and Tok Pisin
  • Training: We teach you how to update your own site, no tech degree needed

Who We Are

Founded and created in 2026, based in beautiful Manus Island. We combine PNG values with modern technology. We understand village internet speeds, power cuts, and how business really works here.

We build for PNG first. If it works in Lae on a K50 phone, it’ll work anywhere.

Our Promise

No tech talk. No delays. No overseas call centers. Just real support from real PNG people who answer WhatsApp at 9PM.

Cholai Tech PNG: Two heads. One lewa. One connected future.
